The Armored Core 6 Roguelike Challenge

The Armored Core 6 Roguelike Challenge is for pilots who really want to spice up their PvE experience with roguelike elements!

This challenge is a journey that takes you through all three timelines, starting with Fires of Raven, then Liberator of Rubicon, and finally Alea Iacta Est.

The idea is that you will be forced to use crazy PvE loadouts that you have never used before, possibly learn something, and most importantly, have fun!

  • Start a new playthrough with the JAILBREAK frame, the starter internal components (BST-G1/P10 booster, FCS-G1/P01 FCS, AG-J-098 JOSO generator), no OS tunings, and no weapons.
  • If you fail a mission for any reason, the run is over. Press the "Mission Failed" button to restart your run. You will keep your OST Chips earned during your run, but lose all of your earned parts and you will restart at the first mission of your current timeline.

You must complete the mission shown on screen in order to progress (you can use the Replay Mission feature in-game).

If you complete the mission, press the "Mission Complete" button (green checkmark on mobile), and follow the prompts.

Every mission has an optional objective, which you can complete for an extra part roll (you can still only choose 1 part, but options are good).

Completing a chapter rewards you with OST Chips, with the later chapters awarding more chips. For longer chapters, there are mid-chapter chip rewards as well. OST Chips persist in your inventory even if you fail a run, making your subsequent runs easier.

Completing a timeline (Fires of Raven, Liberator of Rubicon, Alea Iacta Est) rewards you with OST Chips, a badge, and resets your parts.

Your progress is automatically saved for you whenever you complete a mission. The save data is stored in your browser's local storage, so feel free to close out of the website. As long as you use the same browser, you will have your progress.

Note that clearing your browser cache and cookies may also delete our save data. It's a good idea to periodically download your save data. You can download (and upload) save data by clicking the button in the upper right and following the prompts there.

If you want to transfer your save to another device or browser, simply download your save data and upload it to the new platform.

  • Q: What about the "Destroy the Ice Worm" mission?

    You may use the Stun Needle launcher for this mission (R-Back Unit variant, as in the base game) even if you haven't acquired it through random rewards

  • Q: What about [ALT] missions?

    The mission tab will tell you if you need to do the [ALT] version of a mission by saying [ALT] in the mission title

  • Q: I made it to Liberator of Rubicon/Alea Iacta Est timeline. If I fail a mission now, will I have to start over from Fires of Raven?

    No. You will start at the first mission of your current timeline.

  • Q: What the hell is a Denoiser or a Weevil?

    A: Denoisers are the PCA mechs found in Watchpoint Alpha and Institute City. Weevils are the bipedal C-Weapon MTs that always appear alongside Helianthus machines. (And in case you missed that in-game, Helianthi are the grinder wheels)

  • Q: Is restarting a mission fair game?

    A: Nope. Live with your mistakes and/or die by your mistakes.
