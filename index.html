Roguelike Challenge

    Reset Run?
    Rules
    • Start a new playthrough with the JAILBREAK frame, the starter internal components (BST-G1/P10 booster, FCS-G1/P01 FCS, AG-J-098 JOSO generator), no OS tuning (alt), and no weapons
    • For every mission completed, roll for a new part by clicking "ROLL", with the correct stage you are in selected from the dropdown
    • If you complete the optional objective for a mission, you may roll for an additional part
    • If you fail a mission for any reason, the run is over. Press the "RESET" button to restart your run. Reset your OS tunings in game (not applicable to ALT runs)
    • ALT rule: do not reset OS tuning after mission failure
